Elon Musk: Visionary Entrepreneur

Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur known for his work in the technology and space industries. He co-founded companies such as Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company.

His ambitious projects and innovative ideas have made him one of the most influential figures of the 21st century.

"When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor." - Elon Musk
